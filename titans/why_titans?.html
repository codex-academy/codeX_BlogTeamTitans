WHY ARE TITANS AT CODEX?

CodeX is a great technological platform to learn about one of the most demanding technological sectors of Information Technology, Software Development. Our involvement in the programme took place upon our deepest fascination in technology as a whole. We are part of the programme with the hope of acquiring information, hopefully sufficient, to benefit one self hence contribute in the demand. We are young and innovative thus such exposure might, possible, lead to the elimination of unemployment in this country.

We have great passion in operating systems, gadgets, robots and all new developments as we are leaving in the technological era.

We, therefore, saw the need and became adamant on missing on such opportunity. All that will be brought forth to our attention, by means of continuous learning, we surely fall in the good hands as each one has a vision of making a living out of the programme.
